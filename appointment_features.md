## ⚠️ SECTION 5: TECHNICAL CONSTRAINTS (MANDATORY)

- **Mapping Rule (IMPORTANT)**: 
    - `CONFIRMED` → **Approved** (green)
    - `PENDING` → **Pending** (yellow)
    - `CANCELLED`/`LATE_CANCEL` → **Cancelled** (red)
- **Formatting Rule**:
    - Date: `Oct 24, 2024`
    - Time: `10:00 AM`
- **UI Constraint**:
    - All Modals **MUST** use `z-[100000]` to avoid being covered by the `z-99999` Patient Header.
    - Backdrop **MUST** include `backdrop-blur-sm` for full-page dimming.
- **Table Height**: Keep `min-h-[285px]` on the list container to prevent layout shift.
